Purpose

This project uses informal sources to record incidents of different diseases and indicate potential outbreaks from local to global level. The interactive map shows visual, statistical, and historical data for available diseases and can be used as a preliminary reference for common users and health organizations for further investigations.

Alert sources

Our primary source of information is individual reports submitted by the users of this website.
